12 Jyotirlinga(Shiva temples) in India

Shiva is one of the three main gods in the Hindu pantheon, there are many temples dedicated to his worship in India. There is a reason Shiva is called 'The Devo ke Dev-Mahadev'. The tranquil-looking lord can turn into a destroyer , and can also be a bholenath. He has multiple shades to his personality, and is one god who can truly teach us the wisdom to live life.
